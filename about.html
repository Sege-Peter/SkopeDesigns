Meet Sege Peter

Sege Peter is a passionate and innovative Software Developer & Graphic Designer based in Kenya. As the Founder and CEO of Skope Designs, he blends creativity and technology to design brands and build digital experiences that inspire, engage, and perform.

Sege's expertise spans branding, UI/UX design, website development, digital marketing, and system integration. His mission is to empower startups and organizations through visuals and code that inspire connection, growth, and lasting impact.

With over 2 years of hands-on experience in the creative and tech industry, Sege has successfully delivered impactful solutions using React.js, Laravel, Tailwind CSS, Power BI, and MySQL. His projects help brands establish a strong online presence, improve engagement, and achieve measurable results.
