01.Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information depending on how you interact with our website and services.

1.1 Personal Information

Personal information refers to data that can identify you as an individual. This may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business or company name
  • Billing and payment details (if applicable)
  • Communication messages sent through our website
  • Project details submitted through forms or service requests

You provide this information voluntarily when contacting us, requesting services, or registering for certain features.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our website, we may automatically collect certain technical data such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system and device type
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Referral source and date/time of access

1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to improve user experience — to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and deliver relevant content. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features may not function properly.

02.How We Use Your Information

Skope Designs uses collected information for purposes including:

  • Providing web design, development, branding, and digital services
  • Responding to inquiries and customer support requests
  • Managing project communications with clients
  • Improving website performance and usability
  • Sending service updates or important notices
  • Conducting internal analytics and research
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, or unauthorized activity
  • Complying with legal obligations

We only process your information when necessary and for legitimate business purposes.

03.How We Share Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

3.1 Service Providers

We may work with trusted third-party providers that help operate our services, such as website hosting providers, cloud storage, analytics platforms, payment processors, and email tools. These providers are only given access to information necessary to perform their services.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal proceedings, or governmental requests.

3.3 Business Transfers

If Skope Designs undergoes a merger, acquisition, or business restructuring, user information may be transferred as part of the business assets.

04.Data Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or loss. Measures include:

  • Secure hosting environments
  • Encrypted communication (HTTPS)
  • Access controls for internal systems
  • Regular monitoring for vulnerabilities

Despite these efforts, no system can guarantee complete security.

05.Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, maintain project records, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

06.Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites operated by third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they visit.

07.Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

Requests regarding personal data can be sent to our contact email.

08.Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that such information has been collected unintentionally, we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

09.Changes to This Privacy Policy

Skope Designs may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements, updates to our services, or improvements in privacy practices. The updated policy will always display the most recent revision date.
